ietsnieuws

Interactive game/web toy, where participants are asked to do voiceovers for OpenImages videos. This forms part of the research for Lukas Hulsbergen's thesis (forthcoming). For more info, visit the project page at http://biktorrr.github.io/ietsnieuws/
